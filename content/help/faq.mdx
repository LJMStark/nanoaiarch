## General Questions

### What is Arch AI?

Arch AI is an AI-powered architectural visualization platform that generates photorealistic renders from text descriptions. It's designed for architects, designers, and creators who want to quickly visualize their ideas.

### What can I create with Arch AI?

- Exterior architectural renders
- Interior design visualizations
- Floor plan to 3D conversions
- Sketch to render transformations
- Style transfer applications

### Is my data safe?

Yes. We take data security seriously:
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Images are stored securely on cloud servers
- We never share your data with third parties
- You retain full ownership of your creations

## Account Questions

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for the reset link

### Can I change my email address?

Currently, email addresses cannot be changed after registration. Contact support if you need to migrate to a new email.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings > Account > Delete Account**. This action is irreversible and will permanently delete all your data.

## Technical Questions

### What image formats are supported?

- **Output**: PNG (high quality)
- **Input** (for reference images): PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP

### What's the maximum image resolution?

Generated images are typically 1024x1024 or 1024x576 (16:9) pixels, depending on the aspect ratio selected.

### Why did my generation fail?

Common reasons:
- **Insufficient credits** - Purchase more credits
- **Content policy violation** - Modify your prompt
- **Server timeout** - Try again later
- **Invalid prompt** - Make sure your prompt is descriptive

### How long does generation take?

Most generations complete in seconds, depending on queue and complexity.
