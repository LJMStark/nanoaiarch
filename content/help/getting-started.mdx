## Welcome to Arch AI

Arch AI is an AI-powered architectural visualization platform that transforms your ideas into stunning renders in seconds.

## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Sign Up

Create your account using email or Google sign-in. New users receive **free credits** to get started.

### Step 2: Choose a Template

Browse our collection of 20+ professional templates:
- **Style Transfer** - Transform sketches into photorealistic renders
- **White Model** - Clean architectural model visualization
- **Interior Design** - Room and space visualization
- **Floor Plan** - 2D to 3D floor plan renders

### Step 3: Describe Your Vision

Enter a detailed prompt describing your architectural concept. The more specific, the better:

> "A modern minimalist house with floor-to-ceiling glass windows, white concrete walls, and a flat roof, surrounded by a landscaped garden"

### Step 4: Generate & Refine

Click **Generate** and wait a few seconds. You can then:
- **Chat with AI** to refine your image
- **Download** in high resolution
- **Share** to the public gallery

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** - Include materials, lighting, and atmosphere details
2. **Reference styles** - Mention architectural styles (modernist, brutalist, etc.)
3. **Describe context** - Include surroundings and environment
4. **Iterate** - Use chat to refine your results
