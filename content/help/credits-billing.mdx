## How Credits Work

Arch AI uses a credit-based system. Each image generation consumes credits based on the model used.

### Credit Costs

| Model | Credits per Image |
|-------|------------------|
| Arch AI Pro | 1 credit |

### Getting Credits

**Free Credits**
- New users receive free credits upon registration
- Refer friends to earn bonus credits

**Purchase Credits**
- Buy credit packs for pay-as-you-go usage
- Credits never expire

**Subscription Plans**
- See the Pricing page for current plans and credit allocations

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Your Balance

Check your current credit balance in **Dashboard > Credits**.

### Upgrade or Downgrade

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Choose your new plan

### Cancel Subscription

You can cancel anytime from the billing portal. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

## Payment Methods

We accept:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Alipay & WeChat Pay (China users via Zpay)

## Refund Policy

- Unused credits from credit packs are non-refundable
- Subscription refunds handled on a case-by-case basis
- Contact support for refund requests

## FAQ

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: No, purchased credits never expire.

**Q: Can I transfer credits?**
A: Credits are non-transferable between accounts.

**Q: What happens if I run out of credits mid-generation?**
A: The generation will fail and no credits will be deducted.
