## Quick Start

### 1. Create an Account

Visit Arch AI and create an account. New users receive free trial credits.

### 2. Upload Reference Images

Supported reference image types:
- Architectural sketches and hand-drawn designs
- 3D white model screenshots
- Floor plans and elevations
- Existing building photos (for style reference)

### 3. Describe Your Requirements

Use natural language to describe the desired effect:

```
Modern minimalist detached villa, white exterior walls,
large floor-to-ceiling windows, front yard with greenery
and water features, warm evening light atmosphere
```

### 4. Generate and Iterate

After clicking generate, AI returns the visualization in seconds. Continue the conversation to adjust details:

- "Make the sky more blue"
- "Change the wood to dark oak"
- "Add more warm interior light glow"
